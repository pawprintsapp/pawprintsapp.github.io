PawPrints is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email address, and other user contact info when you create an account.
  • Location Data: We collect and use precise location data to enhance user experience, such as finding nearby playmates.
  • User Content: Any photos or videos you upload to PawPrints.
  • Identifiers: We may collect User ID and Device ID to improve app functionality.
  • Usage Data: Advertising data and app interactions may be collected for analytics and performance improvement.
  • Diagnostics: We collect crash data, performance data, and other diagnostics to enhance app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve PawPrints.
  • Personalize user experiences.
  • Send service-related updates and support messages.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.

3. Data Tracking

We may use location data to track users across apps and websites owned by other companies for analytics and performance enhancements.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent user data. However, we may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party partners who help us operate the app.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect user safety.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices & Rights

You can:

  • Update or delete your account information.
  • Control app permissions through your device settings.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails.

7. Children's Privacy

PawPrints is not intended for users under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify users of significant changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@pawprintsapp.com.
